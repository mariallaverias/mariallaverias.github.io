About me

I'm Maria Llaverias, a full-stack developer with a background in business and strategic project management in a top-ranked business school.

The tech world sparked my curiosity when I decided to create a tech track for business students to help them gain tech literacy and skills to work better with technical teams, while also improving their employability. That curiosity only became bigger over the years, so I decided to transition myself into tech with a Full Stack Development Bootcamp in CodeOp.

Throughout my career, my drive has always been to delight customers. I put my heart into what I do and using my skills to create something valuable for someone is what I find most fulfilling: whether it is an elaborate program or a simple connection or resource that could help something move forward.

My transition to tech has provided me with additional skills to continue to solve problems, to bridge gaps, and to create value...and I can't wait to find a future role in tech that gives me the opportunity to keep on learning and matching this drive.

Projects

Famflock

Full stack app that helps big groups plan trips together, with a focus on families. It streamlines the process of planning and centralizes decision making, itinerary, trip's map, budget and other important relevant information of the group's trip.

Built with: React.js, React Router, React Context, Leaflet, third-party APIs (OpenCage API, Yelp Fusion API) HTML, Bootstrap, CSS, Sass, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, JSON Web Token, Bcrypt.

This two-sided full-stack app is an online directory to find specific beauty supply products for curly and afro hair.
