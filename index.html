About me

I'm Maria Llaverias, a full-stack developer with a career background is in strategic project management in a top-ranked business school.

The tech world sparked my curiosity years ago when I decided to create for my students a tech-business track to help them have gain basic tech literacy to improve their employability and ease out their way to work with technical teams from their business positions. That curiosity became bigger over the years until I finally decided to transition myself into tech this last May with a Full Stack Development Bootcamp in CodeOp.

My career drivers have always been solving challenges creatively and bridging gaps, to create and deliver meaningful and valuable solutions to all parties involved. I would love to find a match to these drivers in a future opportunity in tech.
